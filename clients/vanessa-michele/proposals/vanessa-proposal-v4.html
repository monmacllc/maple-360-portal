Your social media launch plan — ZL Buy a House
Prepared by Quan Zou · Monmac Labs · March 2026

You are a business owner and mother of two daughters. And where others in your position might feel the weight of it all — the business, the caregiving, the daily demands — you chose to do something different.

You asked a different question. Not why me, but what can I create from this?

The answer is a children's coloring book that teaches kids about buying a home. But more than that, it's a record. A legacy. A way of showing your daughters — and every family watching — that the challenges don't define you. What you build in spite of them does.

The families who will connect with this story aren't just real estate buyers. They're parents raising kids with their own challenges. Parents who want to raise entrepreneurial children. Anyone who has ever looked at a hard situation and decided to make something beautiful from it instead.

The book is one manifestation. The story is the real product. And the story is what sells everything.

Who you speak to: Mothers and parents who want to raise entrepreneurial, capable kids. Families navigating challenges of their own. Realtors, title companies, mortgage brokers, home inspectors, and insurance agents — everyone in the real estate ecosystem who wants to build real relationships with real families.

How this needs to feel
Not like you're selling. Like you're sharing your life — and the book is part of it. The community builds because people trust you, not because they saw an ad.
