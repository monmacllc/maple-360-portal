## The North Star

**This is not a book launch. This is a story launch.**

The book is the product. The story is what sells it. Every decision — what to post, who to reach, how to pitch title companies — runs through one filter:
> *"Does this build the story and connection, or is it just selling a book?"*

**Core story elements:**
- A Bulgarian immigrant mom who sees opportunity where others see obstacles
- A niece (Liana) with a chronic condition — not despite her, but WITH her, building something together
- A daughter (Zou) learning that creating things is normal
- A family that grows its own food, makes its own medicine, hunts its own deer — people who actually live what they talk about
- A real estate book born from real life, not a classroom

**The hook that makes people stop:** *Everything I know about life, I'm putting in a book for your kids.*

## Track B: B2B Sales Strategy

### Who to Target First
**Easiest wins (fastest yes):**
1. Title/escrow companies (they gift to realtors anyway — this is a plug-and-play gift)
2. Mortgage brokers (same logic — realtor relationships)
3. Home inspectors (in every transaction — looking for differentiation gifts)

**Who to call on:**
- Start local: Bay Area title companies (Chicago Title, Fidelity, Old Republic, First American, Stewart Title)
- Regional reps who manage multiple offices = one yes → all branches

### The Pitch (Keep It Simple)
> "You spend money on gifts for realtors every quarter. Here's one they'll actually keep and use with their clients. It's a kids' coloring book that explains buying a house — made by a mom who lives in the community, with her kids. It creates human connection at the open house. It's a tax write-off. And you can put your logo on the back cover if you order 500+."

**Key objections answered upfront:**
- "Why would realtors want this?" → Realtor hands it to the family at the open house. The kid remembers. The mom calls them back.
- "Can we co-brand?" → Yes, for orders 500+. Custom back cover with your logo.
- "Do we donate to charity?" → You can designate a % to the Make-A-Wish Foundation (Liana's doctor suggested this — it's a real connection).

### Pricing Structure for B2B
| Order Size | Per Book | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 100–299 | $10 | Minimum bulk order |
| 300–499 | $8.50 | Volume discount |
| 500–999 | $7.50 | Co-branding option available |
| 1,000+ | $6.50 | Custom cover, co-branding included |

**Your cost at 1,000 print:** $2.99 → **$3.51 margin at $6.50 = $3,510 per order**
**Your cost at 500 print:** $3.29 → **$4.21 margin at $7.50 = $2,105 per order**
