## The Product: "ZL — Buy a House" Book

A **children's coloring/illustrated book** featuring characters **Zou** (her daughter) and **Liana** that teaches kids about buying a house — in a fun, family-friendly way.

**Target buyers (B2B):**
- 3M licensed US realtors (primary — gift to clients, use in open houses, holiday gift)
- Mortgage brokers
- Title companies
- Home inspectors

**Sales model:**
- Bulk sales to realtors (50–100 books at a time) — better margins than single-unit
- Avoid Amazon POD (expensive, thin margins) — use print company for bulk orders
- Website for online orders + sweepstakes entry

**Sweepstakes mechanic:**
- Page in book: "Draw your dream house" → upload to website → win prizes
- Prizes: $1,000 travel voucher (1st), $500 (2nd), $200 (10 winners), + branded water bottles featuring Zou & Liana
- Draw date: Zou's birthday (Dec 12) or Liana's birthday
- Dual purpose: drives website traffic + builds email list

## Launch Strategy (Mapped from Call)

### Phase 1 — Story Build (Weeks 1–4)
**Goal:** Get people emotionally invested BEFORE the book is in their hands

- Restart all social media accounts (clean slate)
- Post 3–5x/week — NOT about the book, about the family journey
  - Zou learning about real estate ("Mama, when can I have a boyfriend?" → "When you read 10,000 books")
  - Liana's involvement despite health challenges
  - Behind the scenes of printing, drawing, creating together
  - Small family/health nuggets (home remedies, garden, cooking)
  - Bulgarian community moments
- Every other video/post gets a soft CTA (link in bio → waitlist)
- Primary platforms: **TikTok** (first) → **Instagram** (second)
- Leverage existing circles: Bulgarian Bay Area community → Tony Robbins tribe → friends → neighbors

### Phase 2 — Funnel + Waitlist (Weeks 2–5, overlap)
**Goal:** Capture emails while story builds momentum

- Waitlist landing page live (coordinate with website designer)
- Email opt-in: "Follow our launch journey — get first access + sweepstakes entry"
- Welcome sequence (3–5 emails, automated): Thank you → Why we built this → What's coming → How you can help spread the word → Sweepstakes preview
- Website SEO structure locked (Quan to audit and advise designer)
- Keywords: parenting + real estate + entrepreneurial kids + family legacy

### Phase 3 — Buzz + Pre-Orders (Weeks 4–6)
**Goal:** Convert engaged followers into first buyers + word-of-mouth

- Announce the book officially
- Bulk order outreach begins: realtors, title companies, mortgage brokers (direct sales)
- LinkedIn outreach to real estate professional networks
- Sweepstakes announced: "Draw your dream house, win a family vacation"
- Press/media angle: immigrant mom + disabled niece + family book = authentic story

### Phase 4 — Launch Day
**Goal:** Full release with a moment

- Book ships first batch (bulk + individual)
- Sweepstakes fully live (drawings on site)
- Launch event (optional): small in-person gathering with Bulgarian community + Tony Robbins crew
- Thank sponsors publicly (title companies, mortgage companies willing to co-brand)
