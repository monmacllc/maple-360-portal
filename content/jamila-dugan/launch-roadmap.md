# IP Stewardship Lab — Week-by-Week Launch Roadmap
**Client:** Dr. Jamila Dugan | **Prepared by:** Maple 360 | **February 2026**
**Scope:** Week 0 (kickoff) through Week 12 (M4 retainer month 1 complete)

---

## Reading This Document

- **[MAPLE 360]** = work Maple 360 does internally. Dr. Dugan is not involved.
- **[DR. DUGAN]** = requires Dr. Dugan's time, decision, or approval.
- **[PROOF GATE]** = a milestone review moment. Dr. Dugan sees real work before we proceed.
- **[DECISION POINT]** = Dr. Dugan must make a specific decision for progress to continue.
- **[TOGETHER]** = a call or meeting between Maple 360 and Dr. Dugan.

Time estimates for Dr. Dugan's commitments are included with each task. Total time across the full build (Weeks 0–7) is approximately 15–20 hours spread over 7 weeks — mostly in short bursts of 15–30 minutes.
