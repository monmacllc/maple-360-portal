- TAM: $57M-$107M across 3 segments

## Three Target Segments

### Segment 1: Justice-Oriented Consultants
- DEI/equity consultants with $250K+ revenue, lots of trainings, unpackaged IP
- US: 2,000-4,000 | Canada: 200-400 | Global English: 3,000-5,700
- Pain point: They run dozens of workshops/trainings but their IP isn't codified, protected, or productized
- Buying trigger: Watching frameworks get diluted or co-opted without attribution

### Segment 2: Authors & IP Creators
- Framework holders, named concepts, don't know what to steward/license/productize
- US: 5,000-8,000 | Canada: 500-800 | Global English: 7,000-11,300
- Pain point: They've written the book/created the framework but revenue is 100% active (speaking/consulting)
- Buying trigger: Desire for passive income, seeing others monetize similar ideas

### Segment 3: Transitioning Employees
- Equity-org employees who contributed significant IP, considering independence, asking "what is mine?"
- US: 10,000-20,000 | Canada: 1,000-2,000 | Global English: 13,000-26,000
- Pain point: They've built frameworks inside organizations and don't know what they can take with them
- Buying trigger: Layoffs, DEI program cuts, organizational restructuring, desire for independence

- These displaced professionals NEED IP stewardship education urgently
- Window of opportunity: 12-18 months before market normalizes
