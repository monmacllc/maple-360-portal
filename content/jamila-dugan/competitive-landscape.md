## The Whitespace

ISL sits alone in the Human-led + Stewardship-first quadrant.

### Five Market Gaps ISL Fills:
1. **IP protection for non-legal practitioners** — No program teaches consultants/authors how to identify, codify, and protect their IP without lawyers
2. **Ethical monetization framework** — Stewardship vs extraction as a distinct paradigm from "monetize your expertise"
3. **Justice-oriented IP strategy** — Specifically for BIPOC consultants whose knowledge has historically been extracted
4. **Stewardship as a distinct paradigm** — Not "how to make money from your IP" but "how to ensure your IP travels with integrity"
5. **Intergenerational IP wealth building** — Framing IP protection as legacy and community responsibility
